Under the Hood

EfficientNetB2 Architecture

Our model is built on EfficientNetB2 with Transfer Learning — pretrained on ImageNet and fine-tuned on a curated crop disease dataset. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) extract spatial features from leaf images, identifying disease patterns invisible to the naked eye.

The architecture uses compound scaling to balance network depth, width, and resolution — achieving superior accuracy with fewer parameters compared to traditional models like VGG or ResNet.
